Game Description

Retro Bowl is a pixel-perfect tribute to classic American football games. Take control of your favorite team, manage your roster, and lead them to glory through a challenging season of strategic gameplay.

With its perfect blend of management and arcade-style gameplay, Retro Bowl offers both quick matches and deep career progression that will keep you coming back for "just one more game."

Controls

Offense

  • Drag to aim passes
  • Release to throw
  • Tap player to switch control
  • Swipe to dodge tackles

Management

  • Tap menus to navigate
  • Select plays from playbook
  • Manage team roster
  • Handle team facilities
