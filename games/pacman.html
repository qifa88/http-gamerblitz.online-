Game Description

Pacman is one of the most iconic arcade games ever created. Guide Pacman through the maze, eating dots while avoiding colorful ghosts. Power pellets temporarily turn the tables, allowing you to chase and eat the ghosts for bonus points.

Released in 1980, Pacman revolutionized the gaming industry and remains one of the most recognizable video game characters to this day.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to move Pacman through the maze
  • Eat all the dots to complete each level
  • Avoid the ghosts - they'll cost you a life if they catch you
  • Eat power pellets to turn ghosts blue and vulnerable
  • Eat fruit for bonus points when they appear

Pro Tips & Strategies

  • Learn ghost behavior patterns - each ghost has unique movement
  • Use tunnels to escape when cornered
  • Time your power pellet consumption strategically
  • Clear dots in organized patterns to maximize efficiency
  • Keep track of ghost respawn timing after eating them

Know Your Ghosts

Blinky (Red)

Aggressively chases Pacman directly

Pinky (Pink)

Tries to ambush by moving ahead of Pacman

Inky (Cyan)

Uses complex patterns to trap Pacman

Clyde (Orange)

Moves randomly, unpredictable behavior
