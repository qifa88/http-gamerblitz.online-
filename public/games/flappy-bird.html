Game Description

Flappy Bird is a side-scrolling game where you control a bird, attempting to fly between rows of green pipes without hitting them. Each successful pass through a pair of pipes earns you a point.

With its simple yet challenging gameplay mechanics, Flappy Bird became a global phenomenon, known for being easy to learn but incredibly difficult to master.

How to Play

  • Press SPACE or click/tap to make the bird flap
  • Navigate through the gaps between pipes
  • Avoid touching the pipes or the ground
  • Each pipe passed equals one point
  • Try to beat your high score!

Pro Tips & Strategies

  • Tap rhythmically to maintain steady flight
  • Focus on the next gap, not the bird
  • Keep your taps light and controlled
  • Practice timing between pipes
  • Stay calm and don't panic tap
