Game Description

Tetris is the timeless puzzle game that has captivated players for generations. Arrange falling blocks (tetrominoes) to create complete lines and prevent the blocks from stacking to the top.

With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Tetris continues to challenge players worldwide to improve their spatial awareness and quick decision-making skills.

How to Play

  • Use Left/Right arrows to move pieces horizontally
  • Up arrow rotates the piece
  • Down arrow speeds up the descent
  • Space bar drops the piece instantly
  • Complete lines to clear them and score points

Tips & Tricks

  • Learn to recognize piece patterns
  • Keep the stack as flat as possible
  • Save long pieces for Tetris clears
  • Practice T-spins for bonus points
  • Plan ahead while current piece is falling
