Game Description

Wordle is a daily word puzzle that challenges players to guess a five-letter word in six attempts or less. After each guess, the game provides feedback using colors: green for correct letters in the right position, yellow for correct letters in the wrong position, and gray for letters not in the word.

With a new puzzle available every day, Wordle has become a global phenomenon that brings together word enthusiasts and casual players alike in a shared daily challenge.

How to Play

  • Type a five-letter word and press Enter
  • Green squares indicate correct letters in the right spot
  • Yellow squares show correct letters in the wrong position
  • Gray squares mean those letters aren't in the word
  • You have six attempts to guess the word

Winning Strategies

  • Start with words containing common letters (E, A, R, T, S)
  • Use your first guess to eliminate multiple vowels
  • Pay attention to letter patterns and common word endings
  • Don't repeat letters in your early guesses
  • Keep track of eliminated letters
